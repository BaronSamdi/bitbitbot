Bitrule is Secure!

We respect your Privacy

When working in the Bitcoin eco-system, you have to be careful. Bitrule uses API keys of your exchanges, and DOES NOT require you to submit your personal exchange login information. A new API key can be set from the "Security Center" panel in your MtGox, Bitstamp, BTCE or other exchange account. Bitrule does not store or share any of your personal data and does not hold any of your bitcoins or hard currency nor does it have direct access to them. As an added layer of security, Bitrule does not supply a method for withdrawing funds from your exchange accounts. We believe that safety comes first.
